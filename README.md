# 🧠 AI Interview Weakness Finder

An AI-powered application that analyzes a candidate’s profile against a target job description to identify skill gaps, interview weaknesses, and generate personalized preparation guidance.

🔗 **Live App (View Only):**  
https://partyrock.aws/u/HaithamNageh/vsFYCNHe8/Job-Search-Interview-Weakness-Finder

---

## 🚀 Overview

This tool helps job seekers prepare smarter for interviews by leveraging Generative AI.

Instead of generic advice, it provides **personalized insights** based on:
- Resume
- Job description
- Past interview experiences (optional)
- Self-assessed weak areas (optional)

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- ✅ **Match Score Analysis**  
  Evaluates how well the candidate fits the job requirements

- 📉 **Skill Gap Detection**  
  Identifies missing or weak skills compared to the job description

- ⚠️ **Interview Weakness Identification**  
  Highlights areas where the candidate may struggle during interviews

- 🧭 **Targeted Preparation Guidance**  
  Provides a personalized study and preparation plan

- ❓ **Practice Interview Questions**  
  Generates role-specific technical and behavioral questions

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. Upload your **resume** (PDF, DOCX, etc.)
2. Paste the **job description**
3. (Optional) Add:
   - Past interview experiences
   - Self-assessed weak areas
   - Target role
4. Click **Generate**

The AI analyzes your inputs and produces structured outputs including:
- Match Score
- Skill Gap Analysis
- Preparation Plan
- Practice Questions
